Our Academic Programs

At Happy School, we follow a competency-based curriculum designed to nurture critical thinking and practical skills. Our programs are divided into three key stages:

Primary School (Grades 1-8)

Focusing on foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

  • CBC Integration
  • Foreign Languages (French/German)
  • Digital Literacy Classes

Secondary School (Form 1-4)

Preparing students for higher education and professional career paths.

  • Science & Mathematics Streams
  • Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Business & Technical Studies

Enrichment Programs

Developing the "Whole Child" beyond the traditional classroom.

  • STEM & Robotics Club
  • Music, Drama & Creative Arts
  • Competitive Sports & Athletics

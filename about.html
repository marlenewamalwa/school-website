About Happy School

Founded in 1990, Happy School began with a simple vision: to create a learning environment where every child feels valued, inspired, and challenged. Over the last three decades, we have grown from a small community center into one of Nairobi’s leading educational institutions.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, integrity, and a lifelong passion for learning in every student.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in holistic education, graduating confident citizens who contribute positively to a rapidly changing world.
